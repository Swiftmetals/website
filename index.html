SWIFT METAL & TUBES

SWIFT METAL AND TUBES is based in Mumbai,commenced its operations in 1994. SWIFT METAL AND TUBES is a leading Suppliers & Exporters of Copper & Copper Alloys, Aluminium, Brass, Stainless Steel, Alloy steel & Carbon Steel in shape of tubes,pipes, rods, coils, flat, sections, finned tubes in any size, specification as per BS, IS, DIN, JIS, ASTM & ASME Standards.

Our methodology of working is structured around our belief that to make innovative and superior products, we have to continually bring improvements to the way we work and deliver our products. Delivering most qualitative material, with the bottom rock market price, has leaded to customer's satisfaction. We are the leading suppliers of copper, brass, aluminium, & stainless steel raw materials to various industrial organizations who are engaged in chemical, refrigerations, air-conditioning, heat exchangers, defense, aviation industry, solar panels, condenser coils, food & dairy industy, pharmaceutical equipments, furnitures, petroleum industry. All our products are made from Cu-DHP (Deoxidized High Residual Phosphorous Copper) and Cu-ETP (Electrolytic Tough pitch Copper), using high-grade copper cathode (99.99% purity) which ensures consistent and unmatched quality in all our products.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

Our products are subjected to various quality testing such as Eddy Current test, Hydrostatic test, Ultra sonic test, Radiography, Hardness test, Air under water test, Dimensional test, visual test etc. Quality Control measures include chemical analysis, measurement of mechanical and metallurgical properties and close dimensional tolerances as per applicable codes / standards / specification.
